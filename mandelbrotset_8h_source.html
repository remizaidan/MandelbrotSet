9 /**
10  * @brief The MandelbrotSet class.
11  *
12  * This class handles the maths behind the Mandelbrot Set.
13  * The convergence/divergence information is converted into a
14  * color value by the MandelbrotThemeManager class to fill an image.
15  *
16  * A convergence test is performed for the following series for
17  * different points \f$c\f$ in the complex plane:
18  * \f{
19  * z_0 = 0
20  * z_{n+1} = z_{n}^a + c
21  * \f}
22  * The parameter \f$a\f$ is called the order of the set.
23  * Strictly speaking, this formula handles the Multibrot set which is
24  * the generalization of the Mandelbrot set. The latter being the
25  * particular case where \f$a = 2\f$.
26  *
27  * To construct an image, a different color is assigned for points that
28  * converge and points that diverge. In the latter case, a continuous color
29  * can be assigned based on how fast and how far the series diverge.
30  * The details are documented in MandelbrotThemeManager.
31  */
